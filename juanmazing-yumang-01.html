The Rise of Artificial Intelligence: A New Problem-Solver Has Arrived!

Published on July 24, 2025 | By Jan Laurence B. Yumang

Artificial intelligence (AI) has been widely known around the world. Artificial intelligence is an ability of a computer system to excel at tasks that typically need the presence of human intelligence to complete the task. In addition, it has contributed to several fields of study. For instance, the business field, educational field, medical field, and many more.

The AI is a machine to think and act as a human. This is composed using computer programs and hardware to have the ability of a human. For instance, solving problems, decision-making, learning, and being able to recognize things—AI can either be broad or narrow. Broad AI is for acting like a human for various sets of work or tasks. Meanwhile, Narrow AI focused on certain or specific jobs with a finite ability.

This new type of technology helped individuals to solve numerous problems,from personal to worldwide problems, such as maintaining mental health, physical health, providing suggestions/ideas, education, climate change, disaster responses, healthcare, and logistics—this application is vast and able to continue improving as time goes on, fostering a better society and a better answer for solving a worldwide problem.

To sum it all up, artificial intelligence has been widely known around the world for helping numerous fields of study—it is a computer program and hardware that have the ability to think and act as a person. AI has a broad and narrow category that acts as a human for several tasks and is focused on one job. This technology has helped with several problems, from personal problems to worldwide problems, and it continues to grow and help to solve more problems in our community.
